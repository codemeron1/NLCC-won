# Audio File Needed: yay.wav

## Instructions:
Please download a free "kids yay" or "children cheering" sound effect and save it as `yay.wav` in the `public/audio/` folder.

## Suggested Free Sources:
1. **Freesound.org** - https://freesound.org/search/?q=kids+yay
2. **Pixabay** - https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/search/kids+cheer/
3. **Mixkit** - https://mixkit.co/free-sound-effects/kids/

## Requirements:
- File name: `yay.wav`
- Location: `public/audio/yay.wav`
- Format: WAV or MP3 (rename to .wav)
- Duration: 1-2 seconds
- Content: Kids saying "yay!" or cheering sound

## Alternative:
If you can't find a suitable file, you can use any celebration sound effect and rename it to `yay.wav`.

The CompletionCelebration component will automatically play this audio when a student completes a lesson.
