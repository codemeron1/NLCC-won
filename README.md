# 🌌 NLLC: Neural Link Learning Center

Welcome to the **NLLC Platform**—a premium, immersive 3D educational ecosystem designed to bridge the gap between interactive learning and classroom management. This application provides a specialized experience for Students, Teachers, and Administrators.

---

## 🚀 Getting Started

To access the platform, users must authenticate through the **Neural Link Portal**.

1. **Visit the Landing Page**: Click "Start Mission" or "Portal Login".
2. **Authentication**: 
   - **Students**: Log in using your LRN (Learner Reference Number) or Email.
   - **Teachers/Admins**: Use your assigned faculty credentials.
3. **Neural Sync**: Once authenticated, the system will sync your role-specific data and deliver your custom dashboard.

---

## 🧑‍🎓 Student Experience: The Explorer Path

As a Student, your playground is the **Neural Hub**, where learning is gamified through 3D environments.

### Step 1: Navigating the Hub
- **Adventure Tab**: Explore your active Lessons. Each lesson is presented as a floating card showing your current mastery percentage.
- **Mission Portals**: Access specialized 3D sectors (Mission 01, 02, 03) that contain curated learning paths.
- **Achievements**: Click the Trophy icon to see your earned badges and "Imprint" (Star) count.

### Step 2: Engaging with Lessons
- Click on any **Lesson Card** to enter the immersive study mode.
- **Text-to-Speech**: Hover over text to hear it read aloud by the system AI.
- **Interactive Component**: Use the floating control board to interact with 3D objects related to the lesson.
- **The Challenge**: Complete the lesson to unlock the "Interactive Game"—a 3D experience where you prove your knowledge to earn rewards.

### Step 3: Completing Assignments
- Access the **Assignments Tab** (Clipboard icon) to see tasks dispatched by your teacher.
- **Photo Proof**: When submitting a task, you can upload a photo of your completed work directly from your device as proof of completion.

## 🎮 3D Adventure: The Neural Quest

The **3D Adventure Mode** (accessed via the Gamepad icon) is an immersive mission-based experience where students explore the NLLC's digital ecosystem.

### 🚶 Exploring the World
- **Controls**: Use **WASD** or **Arrow Keys** on your keyboard to move your explorer. On mobile, use the on-screen **D-Pad**.
- **The HUB**: You start in a futuristic 3D hub with floating sectors and glowing missions.

### 🏛️ Mission Portals
Each portal represents a significant learning sector:
- **Mission 01: Adaptive Mind**: Foundation building and cognitive exercises.
- **Mission 02: Po at Opo**: Cultural and values-based interactive learning.
- **Mission 03: Ang Aking Pamilya**: Family-centric lessons and social awareness.

### 🧩 Neural Mini-Games
Inside these missions, you'll encounter specialized mini-games designed to test specific skills:
- **Memory Match**: Train your visual memory by pairing hidden concepts.
- **Word Scramble**: Untangle complex neural sequences into valid words.
- **Missing Letter**: Complete damaged data blocks by finding the correct characters.
- **Syllable Sync**: Break down words into their neural components.

## 👩‍🏫 Teacher Experience: The Instructor Console

Teachers manage the digital classroom and track the neural growth of their students.

### Step 1: Dashboard Analytics
- **Class Overview**: View total students, class average mastery, and real-time activity metrics.
- **Student Tracking**: Click on any student to see their **Detailed Profile**, including their full activity logs, lesson scores, and assignment status.

### Step 2: Content Creation
- **Create Lesson**: Use the "Publish New Lesson" tool to build custom curriculum. Define the title, category (e.g., Reading, Math), and choose a 3D icon.
- **Add Items**: Populate your lessons with specific learning items (words, sentences, or concepts).
- **Class Filtering**: Lessons you create are automatically filtered so only students in your assigned class (e.g., Kinder 1) can see them.

### Step 3: Assignment Management
- **Dispatch Tasks**: Create assignments for your entire class with specific due dates and star rewards.
- **Verify Submissions**: Open the "Completion Metrics" to view student submissions. You can click **"VIEW PROOF"** to see the photos students uploaded as evidence of their work.

---

## ⚡ Administrator Experience: System Command

Administrators maintain the integrity and configuration of the entire NLLC grid.

### Step 1: User Management
- **Create Accounts**: Manually register new Students (with LRN) or Teachers.
- **Edit/Delete**: Update user information or revoke access if necessary.
- **Class Assignment**: Link students and teachers to specific classrooms (Kinder 1, Kinder 2, etc.) to ensure proper data filtering.

### Step 2: System Control
- **Maintenance Mode**: Toggle the global maintenance switch to lock the app for updates.
- **Signup Control**: Enable or disable public self-registration.
- **Global Logs**: Review system-wide activity logs to monitor for any anomalies.

## 🛠️ Technical Stack
- **Frontend**: Next.js (App Router), React, Tailwind CSS.
- **3D Graphics**: Three.js, React Three Fiber, @react-three/drei.
- **Animations**: Framer Motion.
- **Backend**: Next.js API Routes, PostgreSQL.
- **Verification**: Student-uploaded image evidence system.
